## What Makes Luma Different?

**Luma is not an ERP.**
**Luma is a digital business helper that happens to do ERP.**

That means:
- **Event-based** ("something happened"), not structure-based
- **Guided**, not configurable
- **Mobile-first**, one-hand, few taps
- **AI suggests next steps** ‚Äî users never have to think

> If the user ever wonders "what do I do now?" ‚Äî we have failed.

## The Five Core Flows

Everything in Luma starts with one question: **"What happened?"**

### 1. "I did a job"
Log who, what, how long. Luma prepares the invoice.

### 2. "I bought something"
Snap a receipt. Luma reads it and links it to a job.

### 3. "I got paid"
Mark payment received (or let Luma detect it automatically).

### 4. "I need to remember something"
Voice note or quick text. Luma reminds you later.

### 5. "Help me"
Ask Luma what to do next. Get AI-powered suggestions.
